About

"He who opens a school door, closes a prison." - Victor Hugo
iGen Education Trust is a non-profit organisation whose primary objective is to make higher education in South Africa accessible and equitable for every qualifying student. This will ensure that quality education becomes a reality for ALL university students in South Africa regardless of race, gender, social status, economic background, religion or political affiliation. Our desire is to give each student an equal chance to realize their full potential in life and contribute towards an improved quality of life for themselves and their families. In the long term, we see ourselves influencing the design and delivery of education in South Africa. We believe high quality, purpose-driven education can transform students into citizens who are able to contribute meaningfully in the provision of solutions needed to address social, political and economic challenges societies face today.

We Believe


In the power of education to CATALYSE social and economic change.
By enabling EQUITABLE education, we can close the gaps that separate students on the basis of affordability.
Quality tertiary education is vital to SUSTAINING OUR DEMOCRACY and can STRENGTHEN South Africa.

Our promise


We promise to endeavour to serve every South African child who deserves to be at a tertiary institution and needs funding, irrespective of cultural background.
We promise to be TRUTHFUL in the manner in which we conduct ourselves and deliver on our mandate
We promise to not compromise on INTEGRITY, EXCELLENCE and who we are.

Use of funds

Funds collected by iGen are predominantly used to pay for qualifying students studying at any of the tertiary institutions in South Africa. We will start by covering only such students at the 27 universities in South Africa, and as funds grow, we intend to cover students studying selected courses at other tertiary institutions in South Africa and other parts of the world. Surplus funds may also be used to support the advancement of university programmes in order to improve the quality of higher education.
